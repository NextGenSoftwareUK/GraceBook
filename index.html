Karma That Means Something

Unlike any other platform — your behaviour here has real, lasting consequences. Good actions earn karma on the OASIS blockchain. Bad actors lose it. Simple.

EARN KARMA

Every positive action is verified and rewarded. Karma is stored permanently on the OASIS blockchain — it follows your avatar across every world in the Omniverse.

  • Share a supportive, helpful post
  • Leave a kind or uplifting comment
  • Donate to a good cause
  • Share useful tips or advice
  • Complete a Justice League Academy class
  • Join a GHN Love Beam
  • Rescue or adopt an animal via Noah's Ark
  • Help someone in a verified post
  • Mentor another member

KARMA LEVELS

Your karma score unlocks status tiers, granting access to exclusive features, community roles, and eventually jobs within the OASIS ecosystem.

  • 🌱 Seedling — 0–999 karma
  • 🌿 Grower — 1,000–4,999
  • 🌟 Lightworker — 5,000–19,999
  • 💛 Grace Guide — 20,000–49,999
  • ✦ Luminary — 50,000–99,999
  • ♾ Ascendant — 100,000+

⚠
LOSE KARMA

Toxicity, abuse and bad behaviour have real consequences. Repeat offenders lose karma, lose status, and lose access — making GraceBook self-healing.

  • Abusive or threatening comments
  • Spreading misinformation
  • Harassment of any member
  • Spamming or manipulative posts
  • Verified bad-faith reports
